Korean Language Quiz

How do you say "Sorry"?

✖ Wrong ✔ Correct
The correct answer is 죄송합니다(joesonghamnida).

The word for "Thank you" is?

✖ Wrong ✔ Correct
The correct answer is 감사합니다(gamsahamnida).

what does "안녕하세요(Annyeonghaseyo)" mean?

✖ Wrong ✔ Correct
The correct answer is Hello.

The word for "Good bye" is?

✔ Correct ✖ Wrong
The correct answer is 안녕히 계세요(annyeonghi gyeseyo).

what does "잘 지냈어요?(jal jinaesseoyo?)" translates?

✖ Wrong ✔ Correct
The correct answer is How are You.
